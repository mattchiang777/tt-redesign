The Epsilon Chapter disbanded in
the 1960s due to political unrest.
Theta Tau would not be present
on the UC Berkeley campus again
until over 40 years later.
The return of Theta Tau onto
UC Berkeley began with the
help from the Omnicron Gamma
Chapter at UC Davis at the local
Zachary's Pizza on College Avenue.
Since reemerging on January 22nd, 2011,
we currently have 48 active members
and an expanding alumni network
of over 100 members at various
companies throughout the world.

National History
Theta Tau was founded on October 15, 1904 by four engineering
students at the University of Minnesota in Minneapolis as
the Society of Hammer and Tongs.
We are the oldest and largest professional
engineering fraternity in the United States
at over 61 campuses and over 35,000 initiated members.
